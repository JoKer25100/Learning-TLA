Coffee Can Puzzle (Black and White Beans)

* Difficulty: Medium
* Example solution: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coffee_can_problem
* Type: Puzzle

A can contains black and white beans. Repeatedly draw two beans:

    If they are the same color, discard both and add one black bean.
    If different, discard both and add one white bean.

What determines the color of the final bean?
